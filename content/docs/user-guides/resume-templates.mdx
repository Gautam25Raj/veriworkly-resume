# Resume Templates

VeriWorkly separates your **Master Profile** (your raw data) from your **Resume Layout** (how that data is presented). This separation allows you to instantly swap between different visual styles without ever having to re-type or copy-paste your information.

## Real-Time Customization

When you are in the editor, changes to the layout happen instantly via our local-first Zustand state engine. There is zero loading time.

<Cards>
  <Card title="Dynamic Typography" icon={<Settings2 />}>
    Adjust font families and base font sizes. The template automatically scales
    heading proportions and line heights to match.
  </Card>

<Card title="Spacing & Margins" icon={<Settings2 />}>
  Control document margins and intra-section spacing to ensure your resume fits
  perfectly on one or two pages.
</Card>

  <Card title="Color Theming" icon={<Sparkles />}>
    Apply custom primary colors to headings, links, and accents to match your
    personal brand.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Section Ordering" icon={<RefreshCcw />}>
    Drag and drop sections to change their flow. (e.g., move "Education" above
    "Experience" if you are a recent graduate).
  </Card>
</Cards>

<Callout type="info">
  **Data Persistance:** Because your data is decoupled from the design, you can
  click through every single template to preview how it looks. You will never
  lose your content structure.
</Callout>
